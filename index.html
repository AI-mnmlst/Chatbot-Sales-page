FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. If you don't see your question here, feel free to reach out.

How does the AI learn about my business?

When you enter your website URL, our AI automatically crawls your pages, reading all your content including products, services, pricing, FAQs, and policies. It builds a comprehensive knowledge base that allows it to answer questions accurately.

Do I need any coding knowledge?

Not at all! Our system generates a simple embed code that you copy and paste into your website. It works with any website builder including WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Squarespace, and custom HTML sites.

What if the chatbot doesn't know an answer?

If the chatbot encounters a question it can't answer confidently, it will offer to collect the visitor's contact information so you can follow up personally. You can also add custom responses for specific questions.

Can I customize how the chatbot looks?

Absolutely! You can customize colors, upload your logo, set welcome messages, change the chatbot's name, and adjust its conversation style to match your brand perfectly.

How does lead capture work?

The chatbot can collect visitor emails, phone numbers, and custom information during conversations. Leads are automatically sent to your email or directly integrated with popular CRM systems like HubSpot, Salesforce, and Zapier.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. You can cancel anytime during or after the trial with no obligations.

What happens if I exceed my message limit?

If you approach your monthly message limit, we'll notify you in advance. You can upgrade your plan at any time, or we'll continue service at a small overage rate to ensure your visitors are never left without support.

Can I train the chatbot on specific content?

Yes! Beyond automatic website learning, you can manually add custom Q&As, upload documents (PDFs, Word files), and train on specific content to ensure the chatbot handles your most important topics perfectly.
